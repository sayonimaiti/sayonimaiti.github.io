I am a PhD student at [Indian Institute of Science](https://iisc.ac.in/). I work at the intersection of cancer metabolism and metastasis with a central interest in understanding how metabolic adaptations aid in the survival of metastasizing cancer cells.. I am interested in taking an interdisciplinary approach by utilizing principles from mathematics and physics in addition to experimental techniques. 

Areas of Interest
======
Mathematical Oncology, Systems Biology, Signaling-metabolic network modeling, cancer metabolism, Metastasis, Anoikis resistance
